What to bring

You might want to bring a camping chair, some snacks, a warm blanket, and possible some pillows. Other things to consider are:

  • Cocoa
  • Chocolate
  • Beer for the late movie
  • Great friends
  • Soda for the youngsters

We will make sure that vendors are in place for popcorn, soda, and possible some sandwiches.

How this festival came to life

Like all other good things in life - while drinking wine and eating chocolate :)

We simply thought that it would be a great idea to show some good movies for free under the open sky. We applied for a license at the City Office and got it. So here we are :)
